About our Premises

As your “home away from home,” the bed and breakfast rooms offer a flat screen TV, and getting online is easy, with free wifi available. Guests have access to executive lounge access while staying at Las Palmas Guest Lodge. In addition, Las Palmas Guest Lodge offers an on-site restaurant, which will help make your Harare trip additionally gratifying. And, as an added convenience, there is free parking available to guests. Harare has plenty of Italian restaurants. So when you’re here, be sure to check out popular spots like Aroma Caffè, Sorella's Pizzeria & Cafe, and Maestro, which are serving up some great dishes. Plus, during your trip, don't forget to check out an architectural building, such as Eastgate Centre. Las Palmas Guest Lodge puts the best of Harare at your fingertips, making your stay both relaxing and enjoyable
